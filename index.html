Hello and welcome to Vivian's Gourmet Catering! My name is Vivian Andersen and I'm a passionate and professional gourmet chef with 7 years of experience in creating delicious dishes from various cuisines.

I specialize in French cuisine but I can also whip up any recipe on demand, from appetizers to desserts. Whether you're hosting a birthday party, a romantic dinner, or a corporate event, I can make it memorable with my mouthwatering menus and impeccable service.

I will take care of everything for you: from procuring the freshest ingredients to cooking them in your own kitchen to cleaning up afterwards. All you have to do is sit back and enjoy the food with your guests.

I'm also flexible and open to any suggestions or requests that fit within your budget and preferences.

I love exploring new flavors and cultures through food and sharing them with others.

If you're looking for a unique and personalized culinary experience, look no further than Vivian's Gourmet Catering!
